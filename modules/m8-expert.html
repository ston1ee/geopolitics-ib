Module 8: Becoming a Geopolitical Expert

🎓 Synthesizing Knowledge for Global Analysis

8.1 Connecting the Dots

True expertise comes from understanding how the four core units interact:

  • How Power affects Human Rights.
  • The impact of Conflict on Development.
  • How Global Actors navigate Sovereignty.

Analysis Framework

Use the "Multi-perspective" approach:

  • Realism: Focus on state interests and power.
  • Liberalism: Focus on cooperation and international law.
  • Critical Theories: Focus on marginalized voices and inequality.
