Module 5: Global Actors

🔵 The Players on the World Stage

5.1 Major Powers

United States

Current global hegemon, focusing on maintaining the liberal international order and pivoting to the Indo-Pacific.

China

Rising superpower with significant economic influence (Belt and Road Initiative) and growing military capabilities.

European Union

Unique supranational actor emphasizing soft power, trade, and multilateralism.

Russia

Focuses on regional influence, energy exports, and challenging Western-led institutions.

5.2 Rising Middle Powers

Countries that have significant regional influence but are not global superpowers:

  • India: Largest democracy, fast-growing economy, strategic autonomy.
  • Brazil: Regional leader in South America, focus on environmental geopolitics.
  • South Africa: Key voice for the African continent in global forums.

5.3 Non-State Actors

  • Intergovernmental Organizations (IGOs): UN, WTO, NATO.
  • Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs): Amnesty International, Greenpeace.
  • Multinational Corporations (MNCs): Apple, Google, Aramco.
