Quick Concept: Fag Begreber

SUVERÆNITET
Click to flip
A state's supreme authority over its territory and people, free from external interference. (Westphalian principle, 1648)
LEGITIMITET
Click to flip
The perceived rightfulness of a government's authority. Max Weber: Traditional, Charismatic, Legal-Rational.
GEOPOLITIK
Click to flip
How geography (location, resources, terrain) shapes a state's political power and foreign policy decisions.
MAGT
Click to flip
Power: the ability to influence others. Hard power = military/economic coercion. Soft power = cultural attraction (Nye).

A political entity with: defined territory, permanent population, effective government, capacity for foreign relations (Montevideo 1933).
